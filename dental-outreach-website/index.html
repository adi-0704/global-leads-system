FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started. Can't find your answer? Chat with us →

How quickly will I see results? +
Most clinics see their first new patients within 2–3 weeks. Google ranking improvements appear in 4–8 weeks. Review growth starts in the first week as automated requests go out immediately after setup.
Do I need to do anything myself? +
Minimal involvement required. We handle setup, content creation, campaigns, and reporting. You'll spend about 30 minutes per month on a check-in call with your account manager. Everything else runs automatically.
Is my patient data safe and HIPAA compliant? +
Absolutely. We are fully HIPAA and DPDPA compliant. All patient data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We never share data with third parties, and you retain full ownership. We sign a BAA with every clinic before going live.
Do you work with specialist dental clinics? +
Yes. We work with general dentists, orthodontists, oral surgeons, periodontists, implant specialists, cosmetic dentists, and pediatric dentists. Each specialty gets a customised strategy — implant practices need very different campaigns from general dentists.
What if I'm already using another tool? +
DentalReach integrates with Dentally, Practo, ClinicSoftware, Zoho, and most dental PMS platforms. We offer a free migration service to move your existing data over — usually completed within 48 hours of onboarding.
What if I don't see results in 30 days? +
Growth plan subscribers get a full refund — no questions asked — if they don't see measurable improvements in Google visibility or patient enquiries within 30 days. We're that confident in our results.
