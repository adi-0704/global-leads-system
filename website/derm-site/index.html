Good to know

Questions women ask us

Is the clinic private and comfortable for women?
Yes. All consultations are one-on-one with Dr. Sharma, chaperones are welcome, and Saturdays are women-only at the clinic — staff included.
Are treatments safe during pregnancy or breastfeeding?
Many are, many aren't — and we take this seriously. Tell us at booking and every medicine and procedure will be checked for pregnancy and lactation safety.
Will I be pushed into packages or products?
No. Consultation is ₹800 (follow-ups ₹400 within 30 days), procedure costs are quoted in writing, and we prescribe products you can buy anywhere.
Can I consult online first?
Yes — video consultations work well for follow-ups and concerns that can be assessed from photos. Book a slot and you'll receive a video link.
